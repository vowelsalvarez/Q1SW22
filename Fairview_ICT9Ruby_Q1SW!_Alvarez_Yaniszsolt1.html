Overwatch:

    How the game works


  1. The game has multiple Heros and characters with different and unqiue abilities, such as flying, using laser beams, shields, and so much more!



  2. The task of the game is that two teams of six compete in multiple missions where the tasks will change depending on the map or gamemode.



  3. overwatch is also popular due to the representation in game, such as LGBTQ+IA, People of color. The creators of overwatch base the hero's abilities and narrative by their backgrounds, which makes them a very good game with a good story line.
